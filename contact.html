Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common visitor queries regarding tickets, dates, and locations.

Are the events free to attend?

Most cultural highlight exhibitions and folk dances are free for public attendees. However, culinary classes and concerts require paid passes to support local heritage craftsmen.

How can I register as a festival organizer?

You can sign up for an account, select the "Event Organizer" role, and access the dedicated panel where you can list events, track volunteers, and manage ticket check-ins.

Where are the events hosted?

Our physical events are hosted across heritage complexes and cultural plaza grounds as listed in the monthly calendar. Check your registration pass for precise maps.
